Our Services

  • Comprehensive psycho-educational assessment
  • Assessment and diagnosis of Autism Spectrum Disorder
  • Assessment and diagnosis of intellectual disabilities and learning difficulties
  • Assessment and diagnosis of speech disorders and language delay
  • Use and administration of psychological tests (MMPI or similar personality inventories)
  • Use and administration of Stanford–Binet 5 and Leiter International Performance Scale

  • Treatment of speech disorders and language delay in children
  • Treatment of stuttering and speech intelligibility issues
  • Treatment of voice, swallowing disorders and nasality
  • Auditory rehabilitation for hearing impairment and cochlear implant (AVT)
  • Treatment of neurogenic speech disorders (apraxia, aphasia, dysarthria)
  • Rehabilitation of cleft lip and cleft palate cases

  • Developing skills for people of determination
  • Enhancing core psychological processes (attention, concentration, memory, thinking)
  • Improving auditory and visual memory
  • Training students with learning difficulties (reading, writing, math)
  • Early intervention and behavior modification (ADHD/ADD)
  • Rehabilitation for Asperger’s, Rett, Dandy–Walker, and Fragile X

  • Sensory processing challenges and adaptive skills
  • Play skills and social interaction
  • Gross motor developmental delay
  • Fine motor skills development (hand skills)
  • Feeding selectivity management and independence in self-care
  • Visual–motor integration and emotional regulation

  • Treatment of Obsessive–Compulsive Disorder (OCD)
  • Treatment of anxiety disorders and panic attacks
  • Treatment of PTSD and depressive disorders
  • Treatment of somatization and eating disorders
  • Parental guidance and family counseling on child support strategies

Our Mission

We provide comprehensive and specialized care for children with special needs, empowering them to reach their fullest potential.

We believe that every child deserves a chance to succeed, and we work with families through individual plans that ensure continuous progress and development.
